Hobbies

Yet, amidst his dedication to fitness, Muhammad harbors another fervor - cricket. Whether he's on the field, bat in hand, or cheering from the stands, his enthusiasm for the sport knows no bounds. With every swing of the bat and every sprint between the wickets, Muhammad feels the exhilaration of the game coursing through his veins. Balancing his time between pumping iron and perfecting his cricket skills, Muhammad embodies the spirit of determination and athleticism. His journey is a testament to the power of pursuing one's passions, both on and off the field.
